# 🤖 AI Player Quick Start Guide

## Problem Fixed ✅

**Before:** When selecting AI players, the game would show "Waiting for players..." and wouldn't start.

**After:** AI games now start immediately! AI players count as "ready" from the moment you configure them.

---

## How to Play with AI

### 🚀 **Method 1: Quick Play (Fastest!)**

1. Click **"Quick Play vs AI"** button on home screen
2. Game starts immediately with 3 AI opponents (Medium difficulty)
3. No setup, no waiting - instant gameplay!

**Perfect for:** Quick practice, learning the game, playing when you're alone

---

### 🎮 **Method 2: Custom AI Game**

1. Click **"Create Game"** on home screen
2. Configure your game:
   - Choose number of players (4, 6, 8, or 10)
   - **Toggle each seat** between Human and AI
   - Select **AI difficulty** for each AI player (Easy, Medium, Hard)
   - Set trump method and points target
3. Click **"Start Game"**
4. In the lobby, you'll see:
   - ✅ **"Ready to play!"** message
   - All AI players listed with purple badges
   - Green **"Start Game with AI"** button (enabled immediately)
5. Click **"Start Game with AI"** to begin!

**Perfect for:** Custom practice, mixing human and AI players, trying different difficulties

## Troubleshooting

**Q: Game says "Waiting for players" even with AI configured**
- **A:** This should no longer happen! AI players now count as ready immediately. If you still see this, try:
  1. Using Quick Play instead
  2. Refreshing the page
  3. Checking that AI toggle buttons show "AI" (purple, not "Human" blue)

**Q: Can I change AI difficulty during the game?**
- **A:** No, difficulty must be set in the setup screen before starting. You can quit and start a new game with different settings.

**Q: Do I need an internet connection to play with AI?**
- **A:** No! AI runs entirely in your browser. Once the page loads, you can play offline.

**Q: Can AI players be on my team?**
- **A:** Yes! Teams alternate by seat number. Seats 0, 2, 4, etc. are Team A. Seats 1, 3, 5, etc. are Team B.

**Q: How do I play with only humans (no AI)?**
- **A:** In setup, toggle all seats (except yours) to "Human" (blue button). Then share the room code with friends.
